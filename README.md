**Life Expectancy Analysis and Prediction:**

Life Expectancy is a very important indicator of the state of development of any country.

The Global Health Observatory (GHO) data repository under World Health Organization (WHO) has been, over the years, keeping track of the health status and many other related factors for many countries The datasets are made available to public for the purpose of health data analysis. This includes the dataset related to life expectancy and other health factors for 193 countries have been collected, and are made available on WHO data repository website. The dataset is from a period of 2000 to 2015 for all the countries. Information including immunization, mortality, economic, social and other health factors are included. The corresponding economic data was collected from United Nation website. Among all categories of health-related factors only those critical factors were chosen which are more representative. It has been observed that in the past 15 years , there has been a huge development in health sector resulting in improvement of human mortality rates especially in the developing nations in comparison to the past 30 years. Therefore, in this project we have considered data from year 2000-2015 for 193 countries for further analysis. The individual data files have been merged together into a single data-set.

Your mission is :

- What are the top 3 factors that affect Life Expectancy most from this 15-years longitudinal study?
